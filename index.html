Background

We are a web-based platform that provides real-time analysis on drug trafficking operations and locations across the United States. We utilize aggregate data gathered from our sources and inspect areas of high drug overdose in order to create predictive analytics to estimate future affected areas.

  • Graph Analytics
  • Machine Learning
  • Data Mining
